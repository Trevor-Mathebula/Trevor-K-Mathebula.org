    Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

    Ingredients

    1 cup Butter or magerine, softened.

    3/4 cup Sugar.

    3/4 cup brown sugar

    2 Eggs large

    2 teaspoon vanilla

    1/4 cups flavour, unsifted

    1teaspoon Baking Soda

    1/2 teaspoon salt

    1 cup Walnuts or peacans, chopped (optional)

    2 cups KCC Bittersweet 60% Caca Baking chips

      Baking Instructions

    • Heat oven to 375 F.
    • Stir flour with baking soda and salt, set aside.
    • In large mixing bowl beat butter with sugar, and brown sugar at medium speed until creamy and lightened in colour.
    • Add eggs and vanilla at one time.
      Mix on low speed incorporated
    • Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture.
      Stir in nuts abd chocolate chips.
    • Drop by tablespoon into ungreased cookie sheets.
    • Bake for 9 to 11 minutes until chocolate chip cookies are golden brown.
    • Enjoy!
